LUNA

LUNA\SARAJEVO was established in 2002 in Sarajevo, BiH. LUNA\SARAJEVO is the full service advertising/PR agency. We became reliable partners for most of our clients, as we have many years of experience working together with them on many different projects.


LUNA\SARAJEVO is a member of regional TBWA Adriatic Network with over 100 top profile employees. Due to that fact, Sarajevo Office is capable of conducting projects of any size and complexity. Nevertheless, TBWA Adriatic is a member of Global TBWA network, with more than 250 offices, across the world, providing marketing services for clients such are: Pepsi Co, Adidas, Nissan, Sony Playstation etc.

Works

For the past 17,5 years we were developing communication programs, strategies, tactics, tools, monitoring and evaluation mechanisms, communicating with media, governmental and non-governmental organizations, sponsors, medical instituations, creating and implementing PR strategies, fully organizing public campaigns, promotions, events and conferences for main target groups: business community, governmental and non-governmental sectors.

Our knowledge and axperience in realization of many projects, creating communication strategies, providing specially designed communication activities gave new dimension to our advertising world.
